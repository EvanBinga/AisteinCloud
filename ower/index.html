Cloud-based video

platform

Intelligent Cloud System for business data analytics and personnel control may be easily
scaled to all the organization's facilities so business owners can get real-time information
on monitored events from anywhere in the world

Aistein CLOUD is not just a software for building IP-
cameras-based monitoring systems, but a new
standard in video analytics and security

It is an effective cloud-based management system
for business data collection and analysis, equipped
with machine learning capabilities

Artificial Intelligence algorithms reduce a human error
factor in evaluating situations and events,
and facilitate the creation of financial reports through
the flexible integration into accounting tools

Aistein CLOUD autonomously interprets the events
and people's actions, and decides their
"key-action" relevancy

All out-of-the-ordinary events are stored in separate
folders on the cloud server

As a result, business owners always have access to
events and reports that allow them to monitor the
company's performance and prevent financial risks

Computer vision technologies can count new and recurring customers through facial
recognition, monitor personnel compliance with work regulations, identify customer needs
and notify managers of any queues. Besides, AI algorithms are capable of controlling
product quality and detecting defects at the production stage. Cash register transactions
control feature makes it possible to detect fraudulent actions in any organization running
financial accounting: whether it be banks, car service centers, or retail outlets

Why is it profitable?

In a large-scale business environment, cloud technologies offer
a significant cost cutting when compared to traditional
NVR solutions:

Hardware expenses reduction

01

No need to allocate extra money for purchasing NVRs and creating your own server infrastructure

Energy consumption savings

02

Deploying local server facilities not only requires a significant investment into purchasing and maintenance, but it also adds electricity cost to the monthly expense column. With cloud-based solutions, electricity bills are paid by the service provider

Business financial losses reduction

03

Implementation of cloud-based technologies and AI algorithms for accounting and cash register operations control helps to identify fraudulent actions and timely notify the managers. The ability to track goods repositioning contributes to a quick search for the responsible party in case of any damage

Reliability and redundancy
with minimal investment

04

It is necessary to have hardware that ensures the local server system high-availability in order to achieve high redundancy and reliability of data storage. These expenses are also negated when it comes to cloud technology, because uptime in this case is covered by the service provider
