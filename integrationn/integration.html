Integration equipment

Aistein offers its users 3 hardware solutions equipped with tensor chips providing fast
AI algorithms operation for primary video analytics. It reduces the load on the Internet
channel and saves only critical data to the cloud

To deploy the monitoring and analytics system, you need to purchase a set of IP cameras, or connect existing surveillance
cameras to a hardware bridge that analyzes data from video recorders in real time and saves the critical events recordings
to the cloud. The stored data is accessed via proprietary video analytics management platform
