Establishing single platform remote access video surveillance at stores and supermarkets
not only secures the business, but also allows to analyze customers, learn their
preferences, and increase their loyalty

Cash register
monitoring

  • Video analytics system efficiently detects fraudulent
    actions at cash registers
  • All transactions are being analyzed, from cash
    counting to receipt deletion
  • Meanwhile, managerial staff of the store receives
    notifications and reports on all incidents at cash
    register areas and can personally analyze the
    cashier's actions if necessary

Shoplifting prevention

  • Facial recognition system distinguishes suspicious
    customers and in case a theft is recognized by
    surveillance system, their photos are being added to
    the database
  • The recognition is not only face-based, but it also
    relies on body features
  • The security service will be notified in case the thief
    reappears in the store. In addition, the shoplifters
    collected database can be scaled to all stores in the
    franchise

Goods assortment
and availability check

  • The Image detection module controls the fullness of
    the aisles, the appropriate merchandise display, and
    notifies store employees about any gaps or absence
    of goods
  • Also, this module can create reports on the state of
    display cases or shelves at specified intervals, which
    allows to predict the demand for goods, and at the
    same time to analyze the work of store employees
    responsible for merchandising

People movement
heatmaps generation

  • Monitoring the movement of people in the store allows
    to create heat maps of visitors' movements and
    analyze which shelves and products attract the
    greatest interest among potential buyers
  • The sensor can use all available surveillance cameras
    to collect information and at the same time allow to
    exclude employees from the movements statistics.
    For example, filtering them out of the general mass by
    their uniforms

Queue detection

  • Queue detection algorithm for cash register areas
    helps to avoid queues, distribute the load on store
    personnel at checkout points, and increase
    customer loyalty
  • In the case of a queue, manager or backup cashier is
    instantly notified with a suggestion to open another
    checkout line
  • Regular reports can be used to identify times when
    the workload at the cash register increases

The school grounds
perimeter

Video surveillance of parking lots,
playgrounds and walkways on the
school grounds will keep students
safe and detect any attempt
of intruders to cross the
designated area

The school entrance and
security gate

Observation of what is happening
on the porch and at the gate allows
to detect access cards tampering
attempts, incidents between
students, as well as to capture
other conflicts at the turnstiles

Educational institution
premises and hallways

Monitoring the situation inside the
building helps to identify any
abnormal cases: figure out bullies,
inappropriate teachers' behavior
and other situations that can
provoke conflict between students
